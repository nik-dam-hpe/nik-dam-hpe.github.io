About Me

I am a multi-national (English, Finnish & American) graphic designer with a strong appreciation for design and a versatile skill set. Currently in my second year of studying Graphic Design at the University of Leeds, I have experience across branding, advertising, motion design, packaging, UX, and publication design. As the Graphic Design Lead for a student magazine, I manage the visual identity and ensure consistency across all content. Professionally, I intern at Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE), working in Graphic Design and Communications for the UK, Ireland, Middle East, and Africa. My role includes designing engaging on-brand content, managing video production, creating marketing materials, and collaborating on executive communications. I thrive in fast-paced environments, balancing creativity with strategic thinking. Additionally, I contribute to HPE’s Charity and Volunteering Committee, supporting fundraising and community initiatives.
